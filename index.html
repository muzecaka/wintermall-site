Legal Framework & Term Sheet Clauses

The following clauses are term-sheet ready and govern the Hybrid Investment structure. These are provided for investor review prior to formal legal engagement. All final agreements are subject to execution of a binding Investment Agreement.

вҡ пёҸ Important Notice: The following clauses are provided for informational and preliminary review purposes only. They do not constitute a binding legal agreement. All investments are subject to the execution of a formal, legally binding Investment Agreement reviewed by qualified legal counsel on both sides.
Clause 1
Nature of Investment
The Hybrid Investment shall constitute a structured capital instrument providing fixed returns and dividend participation only. This investment does not grant ownership, voting rights, or equity interest in the Company. The Investor's participation is strictly financial in nature and shall be governed by the terms of the executed Hybrid Investment Agreement.
Clause 2
No Automatic Conversion
The Hybrid Investment shall not include any automatic or preferential right to convert into equity securities of the Company at any time during or after the investment period. Any purported conversion right not expressly agreed in writing shall be deemed null and void.
Clause 3
Optional Future Equity Participation
Any future equity participation by the Investor shall be subject to a separate negotiation and shall be priced at the prevailing market valuation at the time of such investment, as determined by an independent valuation or mutual agreement between the parties. Past investment amounts shall not be used as the basis for equity pricing.
Clause 4
Buyback & Exit Rights
The Company shall retain the right to repurchase the Hybrid Investment at any time between Year 5 and Year 7 from the date of initial investment, at the agreed redemption value as specified in the Investment Agreement. Upon redemption, the Investor's participation in the Company shall be deemed fully concluded and discharged.
Clause 5
No Double Participation
Upon redemption of the Hybrid Investment вҖ” whether at maturity, early buyback, or by mutual agreement вҖ” the Investor shall have no further claim to equity, profit share, dividends, or any continuing participation in the Company, its subsidiaries, or affiliated entities, unless otherwise expressly agreed in a separate written instrument executed by both parties.
Clause 6
Governance & Non-Interference
The Hybrid Investor shall have no right to participate in, vote on, or otherwise influence the governance, management, or operational decisions of the Company. Board composition, strategic direction, and day-to-day operations remain exclusively within the authority of the founding team and any formally appointed Board of Directors.
Clause 7
Confidentiality
All information disclosed to the Investor in connection with this opportunity вҖ” including financial projections, operational data, and the terms of this document вҖ” is strictly confidential. The Investor agrees not to disclose any such information to third parties without prior written consent from the Company, and to use such information solely for the purpose of evaluating the investment.
Clause 8
Governing Law & Jurisdiction
This investment and all agreements arising from it shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Lagos State, or resolved through arbitration as mutually agreed in the executed Investment Agreement.
