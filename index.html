What Is Winter Mall?

A fully indoor snow and ice park featuring ice skating, snow slides, snow play zones, food & beverage, and event hosting — installed inside a premium Nigerian mall.

❄️
Indoor Snow & Ice Park

Real snow machines. Real ice. A climate-controlled winter environment maintained at −5°C to −21°C, open 365 days a year regardless of Nigeria's heat.

🏢
Mall-Integrated Model

Installed inside existing premium malls. Revenue-share replaces fixed rent. Lower capital risk, aligned incentives, and immediate built-in foot traffic from Day 1.

High-Margin Asset

₦768M+ projected EBITDA annually. Infrastructure-backed recurring cashflow. 5–7 year structured investor liquidity window. Zero direct competition in West Africa.

Why Winter Mall Exists

Nigeria has never experienced indoor snow. Africa's fastest-growing middle class deserves world-class entertainment — not imported, but homegrown. Winter Mall is an infrastructure-backed, category-defining indoor snow & ice park built for the African market and proudly owned by Africans. First-mover advantage. Zero direct competition.

❄️
First-of-its-Kind

200M+ population. Year-round hot climate. No indoor snow or ice parks. Strong unmet demand for experiential entertainment. Winter Mall introduces a unique, high-demand attraction with zero direct competition in West Africa.

Vision
Pioneer Africa's First Indoor Snow Park Network

To pioneer Africa's first indoor snow park network, building the leading experiential entertainment brand across Nigeria and West Africa.

Mission
Safe. World-Class. Scalable.

To deliver safe, world-class indoor snow experiences through strategic mall partnerships, operational excellence, and scalable entertainment infrastructure.

Values
Disciplined. Authentic. Scalable.

🗓 Roadmap

Our Journey

💡
2015
The Idea

Concept first developed in 2015 after extensive research into indoor snow parks across Asia, the Middle East, and Europe. Identified the gap — and the massive opportunity — in Africa's entertainment landscape.

🔄
2015 – 2024
Evolution & Refinement

The concept went through several phases of research, redesign, and refinement over nearly a decade — continuously updated to keep pace with technology changes, market conditions, and global entertainment trends.

📋
2026
Planning & Fundraising

Financial model completed. ₦7.5B raise structured. Equipment partners identified. Mall negotiations initiated. Full investor playbook and institutional governance framework developed.

🚀
2026
Lagos Launch

Africa's first indoor snow & ice park opens inside a premium Lagos mall. 600m² pilot. 780 daily capacity. 430 modelled daily visitors at 55% occupancy. Full commercial operations from Day 1.

🌍
2028+
West Africa Expansion

Abuja. Port Harcourt. Accra. Scale to 1200m² medium and 2400m² large formats. Each location compounds enterprise value and increases investor returns through the platform model.
